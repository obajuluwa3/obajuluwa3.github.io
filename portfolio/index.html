ABOUT ME

Don't be intimidated by all the letters in my name, "Ayo" will do just fine :).

I’m a firm believer that the best problem solvers are those who have a clear functional understanding of the different aspects of the problems they try to solve. This is why as a graphic designer my desire to gain a fuller functional understanding of web design initially led me to web development. Ever since I started coding, I’ve not only gained a fuller functional understanding of how applications work and learned how to build them, but my love for solving logic puzzles, thinking critically and strong technical background have motivated me pursue it as a career.

I completed a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and built a firm foundation from this degree. I took several programming classes and tutored a programming logic and design class, this experience greatly shaped my ability to be able to read and understand programming languages. While earning my Associates Degree in Graphic Design I also had an opportunity to revisit HTML and CSS. In Design school, learning the creative process helped me to understand the value of proper planning and keeping the need of the client/end user as the central focus.
